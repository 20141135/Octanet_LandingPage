About the Event

Welcome to Dhishan'24, the most-anticipated talk series that promises to be an enlightening journey for all of us. Team Dhishan is here with our eminent speakers.

Smt. Jayanti Kathale

Jayanti Kathale, the owner of 'Purnabramha', converted her passion for food into a successful career. She changed her profession at the peak of her career with Infosys and embraced entrepreneurship to start Purnabramha, an international restaurant chain specializing in authentic regional Maharashtrian cuisine.

Mr. Saurabh Bhosale

Saurabh Bhosale is a social media influencer and motivational speaker. He was working as an associate at the TCS before shifting to this field. He is the founder and CEO of the 'Project Visioncy'. He represented India in the World Youth Summit. He is the perfect combination of public speaker, story teller and humorist.

Mr. Paritosh Anand

Paritosh Anand is the sharp minded and the coolest Entrepreneur. He is recommended by SSB, 3 times with AIR of 6, 88 and 240. He Joined the Armed Forces and left the academy within 22 days to follow his passion. He Runs two businesses, 'We Smile Media' and the 'Dream of the Defence'.

Mr. Sharad Tandale

Sharad Tandale comes from a middle class family with no clear aspirations and goals. He is honoured with 'The Young Entrepreneur Award' by the Prince Charles of the United Kingdom. He is Author of two books 'The Entrepreneur' and 'Ravana: The King of Rakshasa'.
